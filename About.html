Mission statement

To assist in keeping the cultural food of Belize alive worldwide and for generations to come.
We post traditional Belizean recipes.

About us

Many Belizeans who are living abroad know the struggle of wanting to make a tasty Belizean dish but find that they don’t know the recipe. It isn’t posted online, and they don’t know anyone who knows it by heart. This is causing many Belizeans to lose touch with their culture. This website was created to combat this problem. Traditional recipes are posted so that both international citizens and Belizeans can enjoy the wonderful food of Belize.
Belize is a Central American and Caribbean country.
