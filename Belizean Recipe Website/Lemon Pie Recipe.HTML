Lemon Pie.
This pastry requires you to make the crust and the filling. Merngue will also be imcluded. Makes 12-16 slices.
Short Crust pastry
Ingredients
  • 3 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 cup of shortening
  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of margarine
  • 1/3-1/2 cup of ice cold water
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar

Steps
  1. Sift salt and flour in a bowl.
  2. Use two knives or a pastry blender to cut fat into the flour until it resembles breadcrumbs.
  3. Sprinkle ice cold water over the mixture.
  4. Use two knives,forks or a pastry blender to mix until the entire mixture is moistened.
  5. Shape dough into a ball.
  6. Cover with wax paper and put in the refridgerator for 5-30 minutes.
  7. When ready, remove from refridgerator .
  8. Cut into three pieces and roll t about 1/4"
  9. Bake at 450째 F/180째 C.




Meringue
Ingredients
  • 3 egg whites.
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Steps
  1. Beat egg whites with a rotary beater or electric mixer at high speed until the foam stands in peak when the beater is withdrawn slowly
  2. Add 1/2 of the sugar and lime juice. When meringue begins to look glossy and stand in peak, add the other 1/2 sugar. Continue to beat well.
  3. Spread over pie filling sealing it carefully over the crust.




Lemon Pie filling
Ingredients
  • 2 tins condensed milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 4-6 large limes or lemons
  • 1 small evaporated milk
  • Grated rind of lime or lemon

Steps
  1. Break and Separate Eggs. (Put whites aside for meringue)
  2. Beat egg yolk slowly.
  3. Add condensed milk.
  4. Add grated rind, evaporated milk and lime juice.
  5. Add to baked pie crust. (Already cooled)
  6. Add meringue. Cover entire filling
  7. Bake at 350째 F/180째 until light brown.
