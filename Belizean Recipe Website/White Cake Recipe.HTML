White Cake
Makes 12-16 slices
Ingredients

  • 1lb Cake flour
  • 1 lb White Sugar
  • 1 cup of Margarine
  • 5-6 eggs
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 small evaporated milk
  • 1 grated nutmeg
  • Lemon and pineapple essence
  • pinch of salt

Steps
  1. Cream Margarine and sugar untillight and fluffy.
  2. Beat eggs one at a time until very frothy. Add to margarine and sugar. Mix well after each addition.
  3. Add essence as needed. Mix well.
  4. Decrease speed on mixer to low.
  5. Add sifted flour alternatively with milk.Mix well. (Begin and end with flour.)
  6. Bake for 40 minutes at for 350° F/180° or until a tester is inserted and comes out clean.
  7. Cool before removing cake from cake pan.
