Banana Cake
Makes 6-8 slices.
Ingredients

  • 3 medium ripe bananas
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup margarine
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda>
  • pinch of salt




Steps
  1. Cream margarine and sugar
  2. Add lightly beaten eggs, one at a time. Mix well.
  3. Peel and mash bananas.
  4. Add vanilla and bananas to mixure.
  5. Add flour,baking powder, baking soda, nutmeg, cinnamon, and salt.
  6. Put mixture in cake pan.
  7. Bake for 40 minutes at 350° F/180° until tester comes out clean.
